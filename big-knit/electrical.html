Electrical System

In order to robotically knit, we needed a few actuators and sensors. To move the knitting cam preciesely, we needed a stpper motor. To find a refrence position, we needed limit switches. to add advanced knitting functionality, we needed an unkown ammount of servos, placed on the gantry. and of course, everything needs stable power sources.
